## Ingredients
- 150g low sodium soy sauce
- 100g rice vinegar
- 100g allulose
- 50g gochujang
- 25g ginger root
- 25g garlic
- 0.1g sucralose
- 0.25g aspartame
- 10g sesame oil
- 0.5g meat tenderizer

## Cook Time
- **Prep**: 5 mins
- **Yield**: Enough for 1-1.5kg of short rib

## Instructions
1. **Preparation**:
    - Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl or blender.
    - Mix thoroughly until well combined.

## Notes
- This quantity is intended for approximately 1-1.5kg of short rib.

- Calories: 355.8
- Protein: 17.7g
- Carbohydrates: 135.1g
- Fat: 11.5g
- Sodium: 6165.8mg
